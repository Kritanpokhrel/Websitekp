About Me

Get to know the person behind the work

I'm Kritan Pokhrel, a civil engineering student at Paschimanchal Campus with a passion for creative design and technical innovation. Born in 2004, I bring youthful energy and fresh perspectives to every project.

What excites me most is the intersection of engineering and technology - particularly how AI can revolutionize traditional design processes. I'm constantly exploring ways to merge my technical education with my creative skills.
